About

    Originally born in Seoul, South Korea, I was adopted and raised by my loving family in Michigan.

  • Live and Breathe Maize and Blue

    Studied nursing at what is considered one of the foremost research universities in the USA: the Univeristy of Michigan, Ann Arbor. Go BLUE!!

  • Diving into the Healthcare Scene

    After graduating from UofM, I worked on as a registered nurse on the labor and delivery floor, then transitioned to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) to explore the world of Public Health!
